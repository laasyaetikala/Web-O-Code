About

The HealthCare Hospitals Group is a multispecialty HealthCare provider, with 4 hospitals serving 4 cities across 2 states of India. HealthCare Hospitals delivers comprehensive care in 8 specialties in tertiary care settings. Adopting a service-oriented delivery model, HealthCare Hospitals provides cost-effective medical care, with an uncompromising commitment to its core purpose, “To provide care that people trust.”
